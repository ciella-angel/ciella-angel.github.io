Dance for Parkinsons

Dance is a great way to release stress and to help with movement. Dancing to rythm has been correlated with better movement quality becuase it is easier for the brain to move with rythm. Excersize is a great way to emprove mental health, as dopamine, norepinephrine, and serotonin which are all chemicals in the brain that will positively influence yout mood. Support Groups

Support groups are a great way to talk about Parkinson's related issues with others who know exactly what you are talking about. They can help you relieve stress, which can improve overall mental health. Art Therapy

Art therapy is a simple way to manage PD symptoms at home. Art therepy is beneficial becuase it can both help reduce tremors, improve mood, and improve cognition. Any type of art making is beneficial, so it is recomended that you create whatever you are most interested in.
