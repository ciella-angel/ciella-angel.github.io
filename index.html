Our Mission

Nearly one half of all Parkinson’s disease patients experience either anxiety or depression. There is no reason why you should suffer in silence because mental health issues are as valid as a physical symptom. On this site you can find resources to help with managing mental health side effects of Parkinson’s.

More information!

Parkinson's disease is a neurodegenerative disorder that affects dopamine producing neurons in the substantia nigra. Common symptoms include tremors, bradykenesia, limb rigidity, cognition impairments, and depression/anxiety. There is tons of funding and research going into reducing physical symptoms and surgical procedure like deep brain stimulation. Research has suggested that a prominent symptom of PD that reduced quality of life is mental health issues, however, upon diagnosis this symptoms is not often disscussed thoroughly with the patient, and there are not as many recourses. The purpose of this website is to get rid of mental health stigma and to provide recourses and tools for individuals to deal with mental health.
